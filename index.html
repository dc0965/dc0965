DC 0965

Defcon Kuwait aims to provide opportunities for anyone interested in technology to be a part of current emerging Information security space. It is a gathering that welcomes people of all ages and all skill levels to participate, dialog, present, collaborate, build, or create anything that incites mind's eye.

Areas of interest

Being a group of Information security professionals in the Middle East region, we’ve special interest into evolving red team methodologies and treat intelligence approaches to explore new age intrusion detection possibilities.

By working together, sharing knowledge will help us to give the best in protecting Public, Private and Government sectors we bound to.
